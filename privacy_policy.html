At FairDebt Solutions Private Limited, we take the privacy and security of your personal data very seriously. 1. Information Collection

We collect various types of information to provide our services effectively. This information may include:

1.1 Personal Information

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Identification Information: Government-issued identification numbers, date of birth, and other relevant identification details.

1.2 Financial Information

  • Bank Details: Bank account numbers, transaction histories, and loan information.
  • Debt Information: Details regarding outstanding debts, payment histories, and related financial obligations.

1.3 Communication Data

  • Call Logs: Records of communications made through our telecalling services.
  • Chat Transcripts: Logs of interactions through chat support.
  • Emails and Messages: Content of emails, SMS, WhatsApp, and other forms of digital communication related to our services.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and use your data for the following purposes:

2.1 Service Provision

To provide debt recovery, telecalling, chat processes, quality assurance, and customer support services tailored to your specific needs.

2.2 Legal Compliance

To comply with legal obligations, regulatory requirements, and to assist law enforcement when necessary.

2.3 Client Communication

To communicate with clients about services, updates, and relevant information.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Cloud Storage

All client data is stored on secure cloud servers located in India.

3.2 Encryption

Data, both in transit and at rest, is encrypted using industry-standard encryption protocols.

3.3 Access Controls

Access to client data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

4.1 Right to Access
